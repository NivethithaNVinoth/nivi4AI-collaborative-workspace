## Purpose
Identify everyone who can affect or be affected by the project, then define how to engage each.

## Key concepts
- Influence/Interest grid: Manage Closely (high/high), Keep Satisfied (high/low), Keep Informed (low/high), Monitor (low/low).
- RACI: Responsible, Accountable (one per task), Consulted, Informed.
- Change champion: high-influence, high-interest stakeholder who advocates for the project.

## Steps
1. List all stakeholders â€” aim for 8-15.
2. Score each on Influence (1-5) and Interest (1-5).
3. Plot in the 4-quadrant grid and assign engagement strategy.
4. Identify blockers, detractors, disengaged sponsors.
5. Build RACI for 5-8 critical project activities.
6. Recommend communication cadence per quadrant.

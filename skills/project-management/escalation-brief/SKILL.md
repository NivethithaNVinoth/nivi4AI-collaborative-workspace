## Purpose
Give a senior stakeholder everything they need to make a decision in under 3 minutes.
Uses SBAR structure: Situation, Background, Assessment, Recommendation.

## Key concepts
- The ask must be specific: not "we need support" but "we need you to approve X by Friday".
- Options with trade-offs: give 2-3 options, do not just escalate the problem.
- Quantify the impact: "6-week delay and Â£120k cost" beats "serious risk".

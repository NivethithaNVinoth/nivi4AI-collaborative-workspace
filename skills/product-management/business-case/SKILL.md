## Purpose
Give leadership a single document to approve, reject, or redirect an investment.

## Key concepts
- Investment ask: one-time and recurring costs.
- Financial return: revenue uplift, cost avoidance, efficiency gains.
- Payback period: months until cumulative benefit > cumulative cost.
- Strategic fit: links to company OKRs or strategic pillars.
- Risk register: top 3-5 risks with likelihood, impact, mitigation.

## Steps
1. Frame the problem and opportunity in one sentence.
2. State the options considered (build, buy, partner, do nothing).
3. Detail investment, revenue/cost benefit, and payback for recommended option.
4. Build a 3-year P&L or cost-benefit table.
5. Score strategic fit vs company priorities.
6. List top 5 risks with mitigations.
7. State the recommendation and decision required.

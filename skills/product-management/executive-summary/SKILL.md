## Purpose
Give a senior leader everything they need in under 5 minutes.
Uses Amazon working-backwards PR/FAQ format.

## Key concepts
- Lead with the outcome, not what the team did.
- The "So what?" test: every paragraph must survive it.
- FAQ: pre-empt the top 5 leadership questions.

## Steps
1. Headline: what changed and for whom, in 10 words.
2. Sub-headline: the specific benefit in one sentence.
3. Opening paragraph: problem, solution, impact.
4. Body (2-3 paragraphs): how it works, why now, proof points.
5. Quote from a customer or executive.
6. Call to action: what decision is needed from the reader.
7. Optional FAQ: 3-5 anticipated questions with crisp answers.

## Common pitfalls
- Opening with context instead of the point.
- Using internal jargon instead of customer language.
- Writing what the team did, not what the customer gets.
- Burying the ask.

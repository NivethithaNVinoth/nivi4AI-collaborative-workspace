## Purpose
Align the team on what problem is being solved, who has it, what we believe, and how we will test those beliefs before committing to a solution.

## Steps
1. State the opportunity in one sentence: who, what pain, what outcome.
2. Write 4–6 JTBD problem statements: "When [situation], I want to [motivation], so I can [outcome]."
3. List 3–5 core hypotheses (assumption + test method + success signal).
4. Define target personas: name, context, primary pain, current workaround.
5. List known constraints: regulatory, technical, budget, timeline.
6. Propose a research plan: methods, participant profile, timeline, decision gate.
